## Creating IAM User

For this demo, we will be showing how to create an IAM user named ```iamuser_mariyam``` in AWS.
<br>

On the search bar, type ```IAM``` and click the first service that appears.

<img width="742" height="147" alt="2026-05-24_15-54" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/d2dcfebe-68f8-4657-9d9f-eefc52cdb0ea" />
<br>
<br>

Under ```Access Management```, Select ```IAM users```.

<img width="226" height="334" alt="2026-05-24_15-55" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/51e27468-8254-45ee-a309-bee727fca4d8" />
<br>
<br>

Click on ```Create user```.

<img width="110" height="44" alt="2026-05-24_15-56" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/8212c0f0-0699-4aa3-9e25-5030864ba79d" />
<br>
<br>

We will then name it as ```iamuser_mariyam``` then click ```Next```.

<img width="1397" height="413" alt="2026-05-24_15-57" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/ee57b082-9c22-4633-8b83-d789c61475ca" />
<br>
<br>

On the ```Set Permission```, we will just click on ```Next```.

<img width="1422" height="439" alt="2026-05-24_15-59" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/4c5e5981-d27f-4bf6-b4ab-c00c5de1a0ad" />
<br>
<br>

On the ```Review and create```, we will just click ```Create user```.
